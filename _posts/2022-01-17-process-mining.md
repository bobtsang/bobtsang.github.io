## Process Discovery

### Alpha Miner or Alpha Algorithm

Aims to find the following 4 types of relationships base, and convert the event log into a `petri net`

1. Direct Succession or directly follows: `x > y`
2. Causality: `x -> y`
3. Parallel: `x || y`
4. Choice: `x # y`
